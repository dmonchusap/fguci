Mission

Our goal is to host gatherings where fighting gamers can befriend, play, and learn with others. We accomplish this by providing a positive and enriching environment through our weekly sessions and occasional special events, all held at UCI and open to the public. We strive to provide our members with resources necessary to enable their passion for fighting games.

Aside from our sessions or events, members of the FG@UCI community can also interact amongst themselves through our FB page.
